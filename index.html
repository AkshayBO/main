 About Us 
We, shitole Goats Farm, situated at Kusegaon,daund,Pune,Maharashtra are one of the renowned and finest goat farms in the city. Goats are reared for milk and meat. Our range of products include Boer goats, Sojat goats, Jamnapari goats, Sirohi goats, Barbari goats, Beetal goats, etc. Our products are high in demand due to their premium quality and affordable prices. We deliver the goats to our valuable customers as per their demands.
